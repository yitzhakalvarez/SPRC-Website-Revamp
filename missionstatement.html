Mission Statement

The Office of Sponsored Programs & Research Compliance provides administrative support and works collaboratively with the SUNY New Paltz community and affiliates to accomplish these goals:

  • Identifying, pursuing and securing funding opportunities to cultivate campus and regional research initiatives 
  • Managing external funding.
  • Fostering a culture of integrity and compliance.
